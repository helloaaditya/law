Who We Are

Proprietas is a specialised platform dedicated to apartment law, governance, and real estate compliance, with a strong foundation in the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 (KAOA).

We bridge the gap between legal theory and on-ground implementation, helping apartment communities and developers achieve complete legal closure, structured governance, and long-term sustainability.

Our Vision & Approach

Proprietas is a specialised initiative designed to bring clarity, structure, and legal precision into the functioning of apartment communities and real estate projects.

With deep domain expertise in apartment ownership laws and regulatory frameworks, Proprietas delivers end-to-end legal and operational solutions for:

  • Apartment Owners Associations
  • Resident Welfare Bodies
  • Developers & Builders
  • Facility Management Teams

Our Vision: To create legally compliant, transparent, and efficiently governed apartment communities across India.

Our Approach: We combine legal expertise, practical governance models, and real estate compliance frameworks to ensure complete lifecycle support from formation to long-term management.

Services

Association Formation under KAOA

We provide a clear roadmap for forming Associations under the KAOA framework.

Scope includes: Legal structuring and registration, execution of Deed of Declaration, submission of property under KAOA, drafting of bye-laws.

Governance & Committee Structuring

We help build strong governance systems for apartment communities.

Includes: Election processes and board constitution, roles and responsibilities of committee members, general body meeting protocols, voting, quorum, and compliance procedures.

Maintenance & Financial Management

Structured financial systems for sustainable community management.

Key Areas: Maintenance charge structuring, budgeting and fund utilisation, handling defaulters and recovery, vendor contracts and service management, transparency and audit systems.

RERA Compliance Advisory

Ensuring complete compliance with the Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 (RERA).

  • ⚖Project registration and disclosures
  • ⚖Timelines for completion and possession
  • ⚖Escrow and financial compliance
  • ⚖Buyer protection mechanisms
  • ⚖Formation of Association of Allottees

Project Handover & Due Diligence

We ensure a legally secure and structured handover process.

  • ⚖Title and document verification
  • ⚖Approval and sanction checks
  • ⚖Litigation due diligence
  • ⚖UDS and survey verification
  • ⚖Compliance checklist for completion

Legal Risk Management

Preventing disputes through proactive legal structuring.

  • ⚖Sale agreement analysis
  • ⚖Developer obligations
  • ⚖Common pitfalls in apartment projects
  • ⚖Long-term risk mitigation strategies
